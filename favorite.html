Favorite Character: Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main character of One Piece. He is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and is known for his rubber-like abilities gained from eating the Gum-Gum Fruit.

Favorite Arc: Marineford

The Marineford Arc is one of the most intense and emotional arcs in One Piece. It features the war between the Marines and the Whitebeard Pirates, with Luffy trying to save his brother Ace.
